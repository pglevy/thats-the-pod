--- layout: post title: 'Bonus: Hamish Smyth of Standards' canonical_url: https://designbetterpodcast.com/p/hamish-smyth tag: Design Better --- Listen now (47 mins) | Design systems have been on the minds of those of us in the software industry for more than a decade now and for good reason. To create large scale software with a consistent experience, standards are needed to guide contributors. This is not a new problem, though. Before software designers created design systems, brand and print designers created design standards to guide creative collaboration. We had a chance to talk with Hamish Smyth, co-founder of the popular tool Standards, and we relished the opportunity to nerd out with him on this subject.
