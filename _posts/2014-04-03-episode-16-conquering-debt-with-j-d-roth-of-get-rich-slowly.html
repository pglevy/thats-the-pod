Welcome back to another episode of The Art of Manliness Podcast! In this week's edition we talk to J.D. Roth, founder of the personal finance blog, Get Rich Slowly. Money Magazine named Get Rich Slowly the most inspiring money site on the net and one of the top 25 personal finance blogs, as well. J.D. has a new book coming out this spring called Your Money: The Missing Manual and you can pre-order it on Amazon.com.
We talk to J.D. about his journey in paying off $35,000 worth of consumer debt, what personal finacne books inspired him to get his money in control, and what money problems are unique to men.
Thanks J.D. for speaking with us!
